Prompt Timer

Version 1.02 (Build 28)

Prompt Timer is an application designed to challenge your creative writing skills. Using a randomized prompt, your goal is to write as much as you can within the amount of time you allotted yourself

Enter in the desired exercise length using the minutes input, and then tap the play button to begin your challenge.When it begins, will be given a randomized writing prompt followed by the beginning of the countdown. The challenge will end when the clock reaches zero.

During the challenge you may use the pause button to temporarily stop the challenge or the cross button to end the challenge early.

Please rotate your device

This app only supports portrait mode
